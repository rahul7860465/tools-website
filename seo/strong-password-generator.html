FAQs

What makes a password strong?

Length, randomness, and uniqueness are the core factors for stronger passwords.

Should I reuse passwords?

No, each account should have a different password to limit breach impact.

Are symbols necessary?

Including symbols usually increases entropy and makes brute-force attacks harder.
